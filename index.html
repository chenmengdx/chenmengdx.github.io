Biography

Hi, I'm Meng Chen, and my Chinese name is 陈蒙.

I'm currently a Technical Director at JD AI and working with Dr. Xiaodong He. I led a team to bulid dialogue systems in industry and explore innovative AI for Art projects. Specifically, we developed the Alphasales system and Yanxi platform. Alphasales is a shopping assistant which can not only answer questions about product, promotion, and shipping but also recommend products to customers proactively. Yanxi platform is to help ordinary users to build their own conversational bots within several minutes, which covers both text and speech interaction. For AI and Art, we collaborate with Prof. Zhijie Qiu from Central Academy of Fine Arts. In the past few years, we developed the Artistic Mind Map Generator and Emotion-driven Calligraphy Generator, which have been exhibited in several famous international art festivals including Art Electronica Festival 2019, World Internet Conference 2019, Mind the Deep - AI and Art Creation 2019 etc.

Before that, I was a Research Scientist at Dragon Research of Nuance Communications. My research interests mainly focus on computational linguistics and automatic speech recognition.I received my Master of Science Degree from Joint Lab of University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2012. My supervisor was Prof. Helen Meng. I got my Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science from University of Science & Technology Beijing.

In my spare time, I love traveling, reading, watching movies and playing soccer. I'm an amateur soccer player and my soccer career lasts more than 10 years. I'm a loyal fan of Manchester United.

Besides, I'm also serving as academic conference reviewers including AAAI 2021/2022, EMNLP 2020/2021, ACL 2021/2022, NAACL 2022, Interspeech 2022 etc.
